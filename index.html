Hi!

P.V. Sindhu is also known as Pusarla Venkata Sindhu. She is an Indian professional badminton player. She was born in Andhra Pradesh. She was born on July 5, 1995, in Hyderabad. Her parents were volleyball players. They both were at the national level.
P.V Sindhu the first Indian woman to win an Olympic silver medal. She is even one of the two Indian badminton players to ever win an Olympic medal. The other person to ever win an Olympic badminton medal is Saina Nehwal. She has won many awards like the Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan, Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna etc.
I really like P.V. Sindhu because I really like the sport badminton and because I find her very inspiring. I love the way she uses her racquet and hits the shuttle. I love watching her matches.
